About

Who I am and what I am working toward.

I am a network engineer and cybersecurity practitioner with a focus on offensive security, homelab design, and automation. I enjoy building realistic lab environments, tearing systems apart to see how they work, and then using what I learn to improve security and reliability.

My interests cover network architecture, penetration testing, cloud and on premises automation, and AI assisted workflows for both technical and operational tasks. Most of the work here started as something I needed in my own environment, then grew into a reusable tool or repeatable process.

If you want to talk about security testing, network design, or using AI to augment technical work, you can reach me through GitHub or whatever contact method I add here later.
